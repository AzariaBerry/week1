The life of spongebob

Spongebob is an optomistic sponge that lives underwater, in the town Bikini Bottom. Spongebob lives in a pineapple with his pet snail gary. He has 2 neighbors Squidward who lives in a monument head, and patrick who lives under a rock. Spongebobs favorite thing to do is fry cook at the Krusty Krab. Even though he doesnt get paid much he does it because he loves it. Thats dedication, but beyond that he is a very good friend and when times get tough, he keeps smiling and laughing and looks for a solution. Thats what it means to live like spongebob.
